You can enjoy Jeon itself. You can also put a piece of 양파 장아찌 (Onion pickle) on top of Jeon and it will be totally different.

- 깻잎전 Kkaenip-jeon: seasoned minced meat wrapped by perilla leaves
- 동그랑땡 Dong-Geulangttaeng: flat meatball

> Jeon refers to a dish made by seasoning whole, sliced, or minced fish, meat, vegetables, etc., and coating them with wheat flour and egg wash before frying them in oil. Jeon can be made with ingredients such as fish, meat, poultry, seafood, and vegetable, and be served as a banchan, or an anju. - from [Wikipedia](<https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jeon_(food)>)
